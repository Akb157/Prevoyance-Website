Welcome to the Prevoyance
Techfest of ECO, Nirma University

The biggest fest by ECO is here!! Ready to witness the Near Future? You can find events of every wavelength here in this 2 days fest.
From robot race to gaming, from talent hunt to project exhibition we have covered all this time. Win exciting prizes along with fun and learning.
A right mix of academic, technical, co-curricular and social activities to help in the all-round development of the students has always been the aim of us. An experience becomes memorable when you have the people you love, the people you vibe. So do come with as many friends of yours! From team ECO, we invite you all to come learn, enjoy and share some memories with us.
